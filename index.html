Step 1:
Glue the Sport Drink Cap to the CD Apply glue to the bottom of the sports drink cap.

Place the Balloon over the Sport Drink Cap Stretch the opening of the balloon over the top of the sports drink cap.

Step 5:
Release the Hovercraft Place the hovercraft on the ground. Let go of the pinched or twisted neck of the balloon and watch your hovercraft glide across the surface.

When you let go of the balloon on your hovercraft, it zooms away because of Newton's Third Law.
The air inside pushes the balloon one way, and, in return, the balloon pushes back a little in the opposite.
